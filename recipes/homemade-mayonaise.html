Homemade Mayonnaise recipe

Making your own homemade mayonnaise is simple, takes only a couple minutes, and uses only 4 ingredients

Mayonnaise ranges in many colors from white to yellow. This is impacted by the ratio of egg to oil, as well as the type of oil used.

ingredients

  • 1 whole large egg
  • 1-2 tablespoon lemon juice or white wine vinegar
  • 1 cup (or more if needed) neutral flavored oil l, ike refined avocado or canola
  • 1 teaspoon salt, , plus more to taste

Instructions

  1. In the bowl of a blender or food processor, add the egg, salt, and lemon juice (or vinegar).
  2. Turn your blender or food processor to a medium-high speed and start incorporating your oil in a slow and steady stream that is slightly more than a drizzle.
  3. Continue slowly adding the oil until it is completely combined and the homemade mayonnaise is thick and holds it's shape.
